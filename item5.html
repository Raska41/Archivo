Russian Fonts Experiments

For the last six months, I have been studying Russian fonts closely, trying to work on canvas with various tools, experimenting with mixing styles, techniques and materials. Below you will see the search for new forms - probably the most interesting part of the calligrapher. Learn the letter, disassemble it into pieces, assemble as you want, and get something new. This creator's happiness is to create a universe in which everything works according to your laws.

Pavel Drakunov, 2018

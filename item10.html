Coffee Beans

Coffee Beans is a brand new coffee brand. It comes in 3 different flavors from light to dark roast. The idea behind the product is to put out a beautiful, affordable, quality coffee. Roasted in Estonia & designed also in Estonia by Hmmm Creative Studio team: Lead Designer & Illustrator Andreas Roosson, Creative Director Helene Vetik & Project Lead Karl Saluveer.

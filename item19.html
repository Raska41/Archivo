Lonesome Traveler

Of all Jack Kerouac's books I found 'Lonesome Traveler' to be his most transportative and immersive, in particular a chapter where he journals his experiences during the summer of 1956 when he worked as a fire lookout on Desolation Peak in Washington State. Drawing from the startling imagery conjured by his writing and my own travels to the Pacific Northwest I made these visual responses.

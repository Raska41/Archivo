TO LOFOTEN- Tugging at the Heartstrings

"To Lofoten" book is a tribute to Lofoten islands in Norway. A book containing the most important information for a first-time visitor, with beautiful photography by Kristin Folsland Olsen, and minimal, yet playful typographic treatment, inspired by "detached" islands of an archipelago.

Kosta Rakicevic, 2016

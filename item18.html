The Virgin Earth Challenge

I was commissioned to make a film for the Virgin Earth Challenge. Sir Richard Branson set a challenge for companies to find solutions for removing carbon dioxide from the atmosphere, the best solution winning a 25million dollar prize, with an accompanying animation to promote the prize. The animation was made in 2013. Animation by Sean Weston with help from Doug Hindson and Michael Parkin
